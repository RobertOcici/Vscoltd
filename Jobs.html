Established in 2016, Visual Supply Company Limited (Vsco Ltd) is a leading Boutique Collections in Adjumani, West Nile Region, Vsco operates as an apparel store. The Company offers both Men and Women Cloths such as pants, shirts-shirts, dresses, tops, skirts, coats, and jackets, as well as handbags, shoes, jewelries, beauty products, gifts, and accessories.

Retail Sales Associate

Vsco Ltd is committed to a diverse and inclusive environment. External and internal candidates are eligible to apply for this vacancy. For the purpose of the vacancy, internal candidates are considered as first- tier candidates.

Retail Sales Associate Job Responsibilities:

  1. Serves customers by helping them select products.
  2. Drives sales through engagement of customers, suggestive selling, and sharing product knowledge.
  3. Greets and receives customers in a welcoming manner.
  4. Responds to customers’ questions.
  5. Directs customers by escorting them to racks and counters.
  6. Provides outstanding customer service.
  7. Documents sales by creating or updating customer profile records.
  8. Manages financial transactions.
  9. Processes payments by totaling purchases, processing checks, cash, and store or other credit and debit cards.
  10. Alerts management of potential security issues.
  11. Assists with inventory, including receiving and stocking merchandise.
  12. Keeps clientele informed by notifying them of preferred customer sales and future merchandise of potential interest.
